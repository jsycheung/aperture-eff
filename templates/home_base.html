The aperture efficiency is given by $$A_{eff} = \eta_a \frac{\pi}{4} D^2$$
$$\eta_a = \frac{3520}{D^2}\frac{T_a^\prime}{S_\nu}$$ where D is the diameter in meters, \(T_a^\prime\) is the corrected antenna temperature in Kelvin, and \(S_\nu\) is the spectral flux density in Jansky.
